Common Problems - FAQ

My bitcoins haven’t arrived!

Try going through these steps.

1.) Try connecting to wifi or finding a location with a more stable 3G/4G internet connection.

2.) If the problem persists, try restarting your phone.

3.) If the problem persists, check the bitcoin blockchain ledger to see if your transaction has been broadcasted to the bitcoin network. We recommend you check for the transaction using multiple blockchain explorers.

  • Rack wallet
  • BlockTrail
  • TradeBlock
  • Blockr
  • Chain
  • BlockCypher

 

4.) If you can find your transaction, reach out to your wallet provider for further troubleshooting.

5.) If you can’t find your transaction, reach out to us via email with the time and location of your trade.

How do I get the bitcoins off my paper wallet?

Android:

‘Bitcoin Wallet’ (Download Link)

Step 1: Open the app.

Step 2: Click the button towards the top-right of your screen that has three dots stacked on top of each other.

Step 3: Select ‘Sweep paper wallet’.

Step 4: Click the button towards the top-right of your screen that has the image of a camera.

Step 5: Place the QR code on your paper waller receipt in the view of your camera, so that it’s in the middle of your screen, & in the provided box.

Step 6: Ensure the bitcoins are now in your wallet.

‘Mycelium’ (Download Link)

Step 1: Open the app.

Step 2: Click the button towards the top-right of your screen that has three dots stacked on top of each other.

Step 3: Select ‘Cold Storage’.

Step 4: Select ‘QR Code’.

Step 5: Place the QR code on your paper waller receipt in the view of your camera, so that it’s in the middle of your screen, & in the provided box.

Step 6: Ensure the bitcoins are now in your wallet.

For reference, we heavily recommend that you immediately send the bitcoin from this address to another address you control, as anyone in possession of the private keys found on the printed receipt will be able to claim the bitcoin from this address.

‘Airbitz’ (Download Link)

Step 2: Click the button towards the top-left of your screen that has three stripes stacked on top of each other.

Step 3: Select ‘Import Gift Card (Private Key)’.

Step 4: Ensure the bitcoins are now in your wallet.

For reference, we heavily recommend that you immediately send the bitcoin from this address to another address you control, as anyone in possession of the private keys found on the printed receipt will be able to claim the bitcoin from this address.
